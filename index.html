Course Introduction

Cultivating Excellent Product Managers in the AI Era

This course combines AI tools with an all-English (EMI) environment to cultivate product managers with practical skills for multinational corporations. Explore the three pillars that define our curriculum below.

EMI English-taught AI Applications & Practices Project Management
EMI English-taught

English as Medium of Instruction

EMI English-taught

The entire course is conducted in English (EMI), immersing students in a professional international environment. Lectures, discussions, group presentations, and all written deliverables are completed in English — preparing students for careers in global corporations where cross-cultural communication is essential.

AI Applications & Practices

Integrating GenAI into the PM Workflow

Click to learn more
AI Applications & Practices

Students actively leverage GenAI tools, mind-mapping platforms (Coggle), and online learning resources (Coursera) throughout the course. The curriculum emphasises Problem-Based Learning (PBL) and Self-Directed Learning (SDL), guiding students to use AI as a practical co-pilot for research, ideation, and strategic decision-making.

Project Management

From Strategy to Execution

Click to learn more
Project Management

Students learn to integrate project management tools — including Gantt charts, supply chain analysis, and channel strategy — to bring a product idea from concept to market. Through collaborative term projects, they develop career strategy thinking, cross-departmental coordination skills, and the ability to translate business goals into actionable product roadmaps.
